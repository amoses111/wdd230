The Temple Inn & Suites


The Meridian, Idaho temple was announced on April 2, 2011 by President Thomas S. Monson. It is the 158th temple built by the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. The temple interior was built with marble imported from Italy, Egypt and Spain. Some of the temple's designs include the syringa (Idaho' state flower), golds, blues and greens in the wool rugs and stained glass, which reflect the harvest and nature of Idaho.
