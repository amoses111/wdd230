The Temple Inn & Suites


Temple Missionary Accomodations

A full-time temple mission is an incredible experience, not just for the missionaries on this side, but for those receiving the ordinances on the other side as well. Service can be hard though, and our goal is to make serving as fulfilling and rewarding as possible!

Full-time temple missionaries can stay up to two weeks, whereas normal guests are limited to one.

Laundry services are free for our missionary guests.

Missionaries may request the shuttle service to stop by the distribution center either on the way to the temple or on the way back.

Loose button? Ripped seam? Take your damaged clothes to our in-house tailor!

Suites

Our suites all come with either Queen size or King size beds. Free wifi is included and each room features a 50" tv atop a spacious wood cabinet with deep drawers for clothes or additional storage.

Each room has a kitchenette that includes a stove, microwave, overhead cabinets, a toaster oven, and a fridge/freezer combo.

All rooms have a walk-in shower. These showers feature high water pressure and we provide free, luxery soap, shampoo, conditioner, and hand lotion.
