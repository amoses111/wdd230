Target Audience

The target audience is the residents of the Tri-Cities and people who may be looking to move to the city. The Tri-cities is a voice for growth and for many young families. There are tons of opportunities for new business owners and for innovative ideas to flow in this community. There are great schools, tons of activites, and beautiful scenery without being too big or too small of a city.

All are welcome here!


Personas

Here are a few personas who may be most helped by our site and services:


The Bingham Family

Blake Bingham has just finished optometry school and is married with three young girls. He wants to move his family somewhere with fast growing businesses, but he also wants his girls to grow up with good schools and lots of fun activities to enjoy. In his search for the perfect place to live, Blake finds tricitycommerce.com where the FAQ's tell him that the Tri-cities checks off all the boxes he wants in a new home.

- The Tri-cities has been ranked in the top ten places to raise children in the US.
- The South Eastern region of Washington has a great economic outlook and boasts great statistics in start-up companies.
- The Columbia river that runs through the Tri-cities provides beauty and a great enviornment for work and leisure.


Scott Woods

Scott is an engineer. Him and his wife Angie have lived in the Tri-cities their whole lives with their three children. Scott's children have now all grown up and left the house, leaving Scott and Angie all by themselves. Scott loves to spend time with his wife, but she does a lot of service which leaves him with time to himself. He thinks it would be fun to find other engineering professionals in the community, connect with them, and maybe even work on a side project with them. He googles "engineers near me" and it brings him to the Tri-city Chamber website where he finds these events:

- Engineering fair on June 7, 2022
- Local business profesesionals connecting event on June 31, 2022
