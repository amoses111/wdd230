Purpose

The purpose of the Tri-cities Chamber of Commerce Site (tricitycommerce.com) is to provide accessable, useful information about the city to all our fine residents, active businesses and wonderful visitors. The Tri-cities Chamber will organize website areas for:

1. Frequenty Asked Questions

2. General and historic information about the city

3. Creation of city-wide events to connect with each other.

4. Business spotlights

5. Local weather updates
