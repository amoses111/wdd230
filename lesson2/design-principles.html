Old Navy

Visual Hierarchy is the idea that a webpage should be structured in a way that presents content in the order of importance. The first content should be eye catching and should draw the viewer in. In this example, Old Navy does a great job at presenting deals and limited time offers that they hope the shopper utilize. Moheim

Hick's Law is a function between the reaction time that we have to stimuli. In web design this is important because if there is too much stimuli, the reaction time is greater. Hick's law is the idea that less is more. You don't want to overwhelm the viewer and so you should give them less options and make the website simple. The Empathy Experiment
